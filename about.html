Our Story

Three generations of passion for sheep farming and the Rideau breed

Welcome to Red Barn Rideau

Nestled in the heart of the countryside, Red Barn Rideau has been a family-owned and operated sheep ranch for over three generations. Our passion for raising exceptional Rideau sheep began in 1952 when our grandfather, William Thompson, first established the ranch with a small flock of foundation ewes.

The Rideau breed, developed in Canada, is known for its hardy nature, excellent mothering ability, and superior meat production. These sheep thrive in our climate and have become the cornerstone of our breeding program. Our commitment to maintaining the breed's integrity while continuously improving our bloodlines has made us a respected name in the sheep farming community.

Today, under the third generation of family leadership, we continue to uphold the traditions and values that have made our ranch successful while embracing modern breeding techniques and sustainable farming practices.
